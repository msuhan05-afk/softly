The journey

From hive to jar

Seven steps stand between a Himalayan wildflower and your table. Here is every one of them — because a jar of honey should have nothing to hide.

  1. 🌼

    01

    Flower

    It starts with the wild flora of the J&K valleys — tulsi, wildflowers and blossoming trees.

  2. 🐝

    02

    Bee

    Forager bees gather nectar across the valley, flower by flower, trip after trip.

  3. 🛖

    03

    Hive

    Inside the hive, the colony transforms nectar into honey and caps it in wax when it's ready.

  4. 🧑‍🌾

    04

    Harvest

    Beekeepers take only well-capped frames, always leaving the colony plenty for itself.

  5. 🍯

    05

    Extraction

    The honey is spun from the comb and strained to remove wax — nothing more.

  6. 🫙

    06

    Jar

    Raw honey is jarred as-is. No heating it to death, no blending, no additives.

  7. 🏡

    07

    Your Table

    From a Himalayan valley to your home — with nothing added and nothing taken away.

That's the whole process.

No heating to high temperatures. No blending. No additives. If it's not on this page, we don't do it.
