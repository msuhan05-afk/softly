What kind of honey are you looking for?

No shortcuts.
No unnecessary processing.
Just honey.

We believe honey is already perfect when it leaves the hive. So we keep the path from hive to jar deliberately short: harvest, strain, jar. That's it. Raw honey may crystallise over time — that's nature, not a flaw.

The story behind the jar

From hive to jar

Anyone can sell a jar of honey. We'd rather show you the whole journey behind ours.

  1. 🌼

    01

    Flower

    It starts with the wild flora of the J&K valleys — tulsi, wildflowers and blossoming trees.

  2. 🐝

    02

    Bee

    Forager bees gather nectar across the valley, flower by flower, trip after trip.

  3. 🛖

    03

    Hive

    Inside the hive, the colony transforms nectar into honey and caps it in wax when it's ready.

  4. 🧑‍🌾

    04

    Harvest

    Beekeepers take only well-capped frames, always leaving the colony plenty for itself.

  5. 🍯

    05

    Extraction

    The honey is spun from the comb and strained to remove wax — nothing more.

  6. 🫙

    06

    Jar

    Raw honey is jarred as-is. No heating it to death, no blending, no additives.

  7. 🏡

    07

    Your Table

    From a Himalayan valley to your home — with nothing added and nothing taken away.

Origin

Our hives sit in some of the most serene valleys in the Himalayan region, where wild tulsi and diverse mountain flora bloom through the seasons. What the bees forage on is what you taste — the landscape writes the flavour, and we just carry it to the jar.
